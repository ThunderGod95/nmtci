# Chapter 682: World-Piercing Cone Repaired, Nascent Soul Refined

Kunxu Third Layer.

The expeditionary force composed of the Ten Great Universities had gathered, passing through the Grand Void opened by Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole in waves to reach the Kunxu Third Layer. They immediately began constructing defensive fortifications around the Grand Void.

Yu Xinghan was among the ranks, a Civil Engineering student participating in both frontline construction and combat.

Ahead of the Civil Engineering students, Tu Lishan addressed them: "The essence of warfare, much like Civil Engineering, lies in the concept of transportation."

"In Civil Engineering, we transport our own resources to the target location."

"But in war... we transport the enemy's resources to our own territory."

"War isn't about how many you kill or how much you destroy. It's about how much you can bring back."

"Look at those academic overachievers from the Security Department. No matter how skilled they are in combat, they always end up getting dismissed. They know how to fight, but they don't know how to transport. In the end, they achieve nothing."

"Some security captains have passed down their positions for four or five generations, but the lineage ended with him. He has truly failed his ancestors."

"Therefore, you must all remember these essential war strategies..."

Yu Xinghan glanced at Zhang Yu's grayed-out avatar in his Bionic Eye, silently wondering, "Zhang Yu... where exactly did you go?"

For the past two weeks, he had been unable to contact Zhang Yu. He had even discovered that Zhang Yu's company had been handed over to Ye Xingli's management.

According to Yu Xinghan's information, the company hadn't sent any funds to Zhang Yu in a long time.

"Is someone suppressing Zhang Yu?"

Yu Xinghan shook his head, feeling as if the entire situation was shrouded in a dense fog, obscuring the truth.

Yu Xinghan could only send one message to Zhang Yu: "Are you there?"

"With my strength, I can handle three times the weight limit. I'll take more."

Spotting a group of Qi Refining students scavenging in the distance, she extended a dragon claw and snatched up all the trash they were collecting.

As the war raged on, the Ten Great Universities and the Righteous Qi Alliance relentlessly advanced their respective technologies.

Whether it was the reconstruction and management of the Spirit Realm, the flourishing development of the Dark Spirit Realm, or the rapid advancement of techniques for killing enemies and seizing treasures, all efforts were focused on annihilating their opponents and securing greater profits from the war.

Among the many technologies being pushed forward, those related to Spatial Lockdown were clearly the most critical.

When the Second, Third, Fourth, and Fifth Layers were isolated by Spatial Lockdown, and when the Moon in the Second Layer was protected by Spatial Lockdown, the Ten Great Universities found themselves at a disadvantage, suffering repeated setbacks in both morale and strategic positioning.

However, when Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole shattered the Sun and Moon and punched a massive hole through the Spatial Barrier between the Second and Third Layers, the situation reversed dramatically. Waves of Ten Universities' expeditionary forces to the Third Layer reaped substantial profits.

Now, with the latest advancements in Immortal Dao Technology, the war's dynamics had shifted once again.

The Third Layer's landscape was now dotted with an increasing number of research institutes, laboratories, and strongholds, each surrounded by Spatial Barriers.

These Spatial Barriers greatly enhanced the defensive capabilities of the Righteous Qi Alliance's strongholds. They were like a series of stubborn nails, driven firmly into the earth.

Within the Spirit Realm's spatial domain, Divine Monarch Sinful Lotus of Heavenly Demon University scoffed. "Every time we breach an enemy stronghold, it drains vast resources. We're losing more with each battle. How can we possibly sustain this war?"

Divine Monarch Bloodfall nodded in agreement. "If we don't resolve the Spatial Barrier issue soon, our combat space will be gradually compressed. We'll be forced to retreat from our current territories, eventually even from the Third Layer back to the Second Layer... If we keep retreating like this, we might as well withdraw to our respective universities."

As the Soul Transformation cultivators from the Ten Great Universities voiced their concerns, their gazes subtly converged on Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole.

Magnetic Pole, acutely aware of their attention, remained silent, as if she hadn't heard a word.

Meanwhile, her mind replayed scenes from the past four months.

When she first shattered the Spatial Lockdown between the Second and Third Layers, the Soul Transformation cultivators from all the universities had treated her with utmost respect. When she proposed repairing the World-Piercing Cone, though some harbored private reservations, none dared voice opposition.

But as time passed, calls began to circulate within the Spirit Realm urging Magnetic Pole to share her Spatial Technology. This would allow the universities to launch multi-pronged assaults on the Third Layer, rather than relying solely on the Grand Void for entry and exit.

Two months passed, and the voices in the Spirit Realm grew louder. Soul Transformation Divine Monarchs began visiting, subtly and not-so-subtly mentioning Spatial Technology.

A month ago, Soul Transformation cultivators started proposing the sharing of Spatial Technology at every meeting. Though Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole directly rejected these proposals, she could sense the growing discontent among the Soul Transformation cultivators.

Now, with the Righteous Qi Alliance's Spatial Technology spreading, the Soul Transformation cultivators' dissatisfaction had reached a new peak. Today's meeting began with a barrage of attacks against Magnetic Pole.

As the Divine Monarchs spoke in the Spirit Realm's Space, Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole didn't even lift an eyelid. She merely said calmly, "The World-Piercing Cone is still under repair. Once the magical artifact is fixed, I will begin breaking through the various Spatial Barriers."

Divine Monarch Sinful Lotus roared, "Magnetic Pole! You've been saying that for four months!"

Divine Monarch Bloodfall said, "The battle situation is critical. How much longer must we wait?"

Divine Monarch Great Devourer murmured, "How many people are losing money every day? If this continues, I fear all Ten Greats will go bankrupt."

Divine Monarch Thousand Puppets of Immortal Armaments University chimed in, "Magnetic Pole, losing some money is a minor matter, but this battle concerns the safety of all our universities and the future of billions of lives. We can't afford to delay."

Looking at Divine Monarch Thousand Puppets, the former director of Immortal Armaments University's Artifact Refinement Department, who had been involved in over two thousand copyright disputes in just three years and was known as one of the university's "Kings of Plagiarism," Magnetic Pole couldn't help but laugh.

"Billions of lives?" Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole scoffed. "Don't make me laugh, Thousand Puppets. I bet you're just waiting to copy my World-Piercing Cone, aren't you? Even if I let you copy it, would you even understand it?"

Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole abruptly stood, and nine Nine Heavens Primordial Yang Pagodas blazed to life behind her, forming a great formation that suppressed the Spirit Realm space before them.

The Soul Transformation Divine Monarchs present felt their hearts leap.

Divine Monarch Sinful Lotus thought to herself, "This woman stored so many Nine Heavens Primordial Yang Pagodas in the Third Layer? And she can even form a great formation with them..."

"Billions of lives..." Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole said coldly. "If you're so anxious, go ahead and panic. I couldn't care less."

"I said the World-Piercing Cone is under repair, and that's that."

"Whether you believe me or not is none of my business."

"At worst, I'll withdraw Myriad Laws University and leave the Third Layer for you all to fight over."

The Soul Transformation Divine Monarch from Vajra University advised, "Magnetic Pole, we certainly believe you. But the situation is tense, and everyone's anxious."

"Could you give us an estimate? How much longer will it take?"

Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole waved her wide sleeves and turned to leave. "Soon. At most, another month or two. Just wait."

Watching her retreating figure, Divine Monarch Thousand Puppets from Immortal Armaments University fumed, "Did you see her attitude?! You'd think she single-handedly conquered the Third Layer!"

The meeting dissolved in discord. The assembled Soul Transformation Divine Monarchs exchanged glances, their dissatisfaction growing.

As Divine Monarch Magnetic Pole departed, she thought to herself, "It's a good thing I took the blame."

"If they had known Zhang Yu broke through the Spatial Barrier, they would have been borrowing him for every battlefield these past few months, leaving him no time to advance."

"But..."

Looking toward the Grand Void, she mused, "The Righteous Qi Alliance has been gathering strength for so long. They're likely preparing a major counteroffensive."

"The pressure will only intensify in the coming month."

On the other side of the battlefield, Yu Xinghan could sense the Ten Great Universities' tactics growing increasingly aggressive as the war turned against them.

He watched as individuals were herded into the battlefield slaughterhouse jointly established by Heavenly Demon University and Netherworld University, only to be butchered alive and transformed into various corpse components.

Some of these components were dumped into Netherworld University's Blood Pool, others were used to construct defensive fortifications, and still others were further processed into magical artifacts for students to wield in battle.

Yu Xinghan knew that while these individuals were labeled as prisoners of war, some were not.

He even suspected that if the war continued to deteriorate, the Four Heavenly Demon Schools' actions might become even more extreme.

After all, he had recently overheard Heavenly Demon University students discussing proposals from their higher-ups: mass-producing "human farms" to breed prefabricated humans with a three-month lifespan, unleashing Earthly Fiend Qi, and employing tactics that would cause irreversible damage and permanent pollution to the Third Layer.

Glancing at Zhang Yu's still-grayed-out avatar in his communication log, Yu Xinghan thought to himself, "It's been four months already..."

In the next moment, a notification from his superiors appeared in his bionic eye.

"Building defensive fortifications, are we?" Yu Xinghan mused. "If they're building fortifications, should we do the same?"

In the days that followed, the Third Layer's landscape became a jagged network of interlocking defensive lines, each side constructing massive fortifications that devoured countless resources and reduced wealth to ashes, like grinding machines.
